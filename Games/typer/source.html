Typer

Typer is a tower-defense-like typing game. Characters may be treated as case sensitive or case insensitive depending on the selected mode. Typing a wrong character decreases your score. Press the button below to open and play the game.

Play Typer
Click the button to open the game. Use ESC to pause.
