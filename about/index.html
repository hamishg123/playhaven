About

PlayHaven.site is a free online platform where players can discover and enjoy a growing collection of HTML5 and Unity games — all playable instantly in your browser.

Our goal is to give indie developers and hobbyists a place to share their creations and reach more players without needing complex uploads or publishing accounts.

Whether you're developing your first small project or a full game, PlayHaven helps make your work accessible to everyone.
